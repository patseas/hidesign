Reviews Airbnb

Fantastic apartment in Athens! 
Great location - quiet neighborhood near the top of the hill. Great breezes through the apartment - light and airy. Amazing views over Athens. Great Greek takeaway food options nearby (To Omorfo especially). The best up market shopping in Athens nearby too if you're into that (I miraculously managed to steer my wife past these so that we could see some historical sites!). Great layout to apartment and tastefully decorated, everything that you need and lovely breakfast foods provided by Pagona - thank you! Highly recommend using Pagona's friend for airport transfers. Great for our one year old - Pagona even had a high chair - something that I would recommend to all owners, as much to protect your furniture as for our convenience! Pagona even arranged for some items that we accidentally left to be sent to us. We loved our stay in Athens, and could have stayed longer, and a lot of this was due to this great apartment. Thanks Pagona, we will be back!

Traditional Stoned House In Kea
VINCENT L. - AUGust 2015

Our favorite place in Greece ! 
This was our second time in this charming house! Sometimes you are disappointed as it doesn't look exactly like in your memory. But we found it like when we left 2 years ago. Still nicely decorated/furnished and fully equiped. The kids (14, 12, 7) and the cat liked it too. The location is perfect: within the harbor town of Korissia, nearby shops and tavernas - but far enough from the dock to be quiet too. The upper terrace with the bbq is a big plus and the view is outstanding. You don't even need a car if you want to relax. Bus and taxi station is nearby. Everything you need in the house (from hot water to washing machine and much more...) perfectly works and the mattresses are really comfortable. Hara is a great owner, always available if necessary (by e-mail, phone or text message) and very accommodating (time of arrival, departure) To make it short I would say we feel at home at Hara's . We will definitely come back again. Thanks and see you next time!
