about us

We are a family company situated in Athens. Our aim is to offer you a home rental experience that combines the comforts of a luxury hotel and at the same time offers you the hospitality that only a local can provide you. Our houses are fully furnished, stylishly decorated and carefully placed in order to give you the best starting point to discover our beautiful country.
We have excellent knowledge of the areas where we offer you our homes and we are always open to help you make the best decisions to full enjoy your stay.

We are not just an online booking system

Hidesign Athens was created by a group of engineers and architects. Their work is about buying, designing, renovating and renting apartments which are in excellent locations in Greece. Our team is open to suggestions in order to take into consideration other apartments that could be part of hidesign athens if the qualify. We are here to support owners in generating the maximum returns from their assets. Owners will increase their rental income, save time but also feel proud of their properties as guests will experience a high quality stay and great service.
