Villa In Sounio

SEA VIEW TO THE AEGEAN

  The villa is located on a hill, 50m from the coast, on 4000sqm of land facing the Southwest Aegean sea and the temple of Poseidon in Cape Sounio. It is a private residence of 250sq m, designed by Manetas a well known Greek architect, and decorated by Stelios Anastasiadis’ painting on the main atrium. It combines the principles of modern architecture with the ones of Greek traditional Aegean architecture keeping its dimensions small and human, fitting nicely with the nature of the 4000sq m land surrounding it.

In the interior of the house the simple built beds and sofas create a fully functional environment for relaxed living. The out of season months provide a real escape to peace, tranquillity and relaxation. Around the house we have no car traffic. This makes our community very private, quite and safe. In 100 m distance from the house you can dive to the sea and snorkel in the crystal waters from a small remote sandy beach with a rock cave that provides shade.

In 5kms (10min) you can reach the Cape of Sounio - the southest tip of Attica where you can visit the ancient Poseidon temple and enjoy the magical view of Saronikos Bay. Athens historical center (where most of the major Greek museums are) & shopping centre in a 52kms distance (50min) driving through the 'Attica Highway' or 60km if you prefer the coastal highway with it's magical western sea view. You may also use the 'Metro train' in case somebody does not want to drive downtown. Other Activities: basketball, diving, snorkelling
