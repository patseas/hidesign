Tube - Luxury Apartment In Kolonaki

LET'S SPEAK ABOUT OUR APARTMENT

TUBE is a 80m2 flat designed and owned by an architect. It is situated in Kolonaki, the most elegant and secure area of Athens, 50m from Kolonaki sqr. Originally an architectural office has been transformed in 2011 into a modern, fully equipped home. Furniture and lighting designed in plain lines and manufactured by wood and iron, mix with natural fabrics creating warm and interesting spaces. The dining room and the living room with the fireplace open to plant filled terrace ideal for relaxing or dining.Enjoy the luxury, comfort and elegance while staying in our residence.

The simplicity in the design of the flat and furniture mix with the warmth of the natural wood, the soft fabrics and the rough metal lightings to give the unique character of the apartment. Take your breakfast in the dining table (up to 8 persons) or in the front plant balcony.

Take a walk round Athens and visit Museum Benaki in Pireus Str or the new Museum of Acropolis. If you are in the mood visit a theatre, a cinema or have a coffee and a drink, all located one step from the house.

I am sure it will be a wonderful escape to spend some days in our lodging. I hope that TUBE will be an additional adventure in your voyage with its hand crafted lighting and furniture designed specially for the flat. Please do not hesitate to contact me if you require any further information or if you have any special requests. We look forward to welcoming you and helping you have a nice time in our city.
