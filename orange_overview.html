ORANGE -  SUNNY AND FRIENDLY APARTMENS

LET'S SPEAK ABOUT OUR APARTMENT

Orange is a 50m2 flat designed and owned by an architect.It is situated in Zografou , 10minutes far from Center of Athens. Furniture and lighting designed in plain lines and manufactured by wood and iron, mix with natural fabrics creating warm and interesting spaces. The dining room and the living room is for relaxing or dining.

Enjoy the comfort and elegance while staying in our residence.

The simplicity in the design of the flat and furniture mix with the warmth of the natural wood, the soft fabrics and the rough metal lightings to give the unique character of the apartment. In front of the apartment there is a Park to take a walk or jogging every morning . Museum Benaki in Pireus Str or the new Museum of Acropolis is 15 minutes far from the apartment. If you are in the mood visit a theater, a cinema or have a coffee and a drink, all located very close from the house.
