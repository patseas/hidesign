Hidesign Luxury Studio In Kolonaki

LET'S SPEAK ABOUT OUR APARTMENT

Hidesign Studio 02 is a new apartment in Kolonaki area.

It is a 42m 2 studio with a beautiful terrace in the center of Athens. It stands on the top of the floor of a residence building on a quiet street filled with beautiful trees. The feeling of the neighborhood is as it is situated in the out squirts of the city but at the same time it is in an unexpected small distance from Evangelismos or Megaro Mousikis Metro Station ( 3 minutes walking distance ) that makes living very simple and easy.

When we design the house we tried to combine the feeling created from the high ceilings with the beautiful gipsum finishes and the old wooden floor with our designed metal and wooden furniture to make an interesting apartment to leave in. We used old colorful tiles for the kitchen worktop and plaster and walnut for the bathroom. Pictures from the old Athens and the way people lived 50 years ago, lead by metal tube lambs , metal table and chairs with walnut wood designed for breakfast and other meals.

We hope you will have an unforgettable time in Athens and that our apartment will be a part of your comfort and entertainment.
