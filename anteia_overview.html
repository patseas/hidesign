Anteia - Amazing View Apartment In Kolonaki

LET'S SPEAK ABOUT OUR APARTMENT

 ANTEIA is a 90m2  flat designed and owned by an architect. It is situated in Kolonaki , the most elegance and secure area of Athens, 250m from Kolonaki square, minutes walking to Evagelismos Metro Station and 15 minutes to Syntagma square.

Built in 1977 it has been renovated in 2011 and transformed into a modern fully equipped home .The carefully preserved original character of the apartment combined with antique and design furniture and  lighting mix with the amazing view of the living room , the warmth of the fireplace and the garden of the back terrace creating a sense of interest and tranquility .

  The house is the perfect base for exploring the city, shopping or hanging out in coffee shops, restaurants and bars in Kolonaki, Psiri and other areas in the center of Athens located in arm’s length from the apartment.

I am sure it will be a wonderful escape to spend some days in our lodging. I hope you will enjoy sitting in the living room, watching the mountain Imitos and the sea and dining in the quietness of the plant filled terrace. Please do not hesitate to contact me if you require any further information or if you have any special requests.  We look forward to welcoming you and helping you have a nice time.
